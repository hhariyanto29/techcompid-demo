User Walkthrough · UMKM Merchant

The merchant journey, scan to settle.

UMKM merchants scan customer QRs from their POS device — like a Starbucks barista. Discovery Mall collects all payments centrally, then settles to merchant bank accounts daily.

01
Open the POS app.

Each UMKM merchant opens the Festival POS on their device (phone/tablet provided or BYOD). They see scanner ready + settlement summary.

No daily code needed. Just scan customer QRs directly — Starbucks-style.

02
Customer arrives at the stand.

A festival member walks up and orders. The merchant prepares the bill as usual.

Bill preparation stays the same as before — manual or POS-based pricing.

03
Scan customer's Festival Pass.

When customer is ready to pay, they show their QR on phone. Merchant scans it with the POS camera. Customer name + member ID appear instantly.

Just one scan: identifies the customer and links the upcoming transaction to their account.
Merchant POS
discoverykutafestival.id/merchant/pos
04
Enter the bill amount.

Merchant types in the transaction amount. The POS shows how many points the customer will earn. Tap "Send Payment Request" — request is pushed to customer's phone.

One field, one tap. The amount is the only thing the merchant inputs.
discoverykutafestival.id/merchant/pos/charge
05
Customer pays.

Customer picks their payment method (QRIS, GoPay, OVO, credit card, etc.) and confirms. Money flows to Discovery Mall as the merchant of record. The transaction appears as "settled" on the merchant's dashboard.

Discovery Mall is the merchant of record. All payments collect centrally first.

06
Track daily settlement.

Merchant sees their pending settlement balance grow throughout the day. Next payout date is shown clearly. Settlement runs daily — funds reach the merchant's bank account by morning.

Daily payout: Discovery Mall settles net of MDR fee to merchant's registered bank account every day at 09:00 WITA.
discoverykutafestival.id/merchant/settlement
End of Merchant Flow

Designed around cashier-style scan.

Like Starbucks: the merchant is the active party at the counter. They scan the customer's QR and enter the bill. The customer is passive — they only approve the payment request on their phone. Discovery Mall handles all the money movement and pays merchants daily.
