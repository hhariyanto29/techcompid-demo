User Walkthrough · Customer / Member

The member journey, Starbucks-style.

Show QR, get scanned, receive payment request, approve. 8 steps from first-time visitor to rewarded member — exactly like Starbucks Rewards.

8
Steps in Journey
1
QR — Festival Pass
∞
Points to Earn
01
Discover the festival.

Customer lands on the festival microsite. They see the venue map, learn how the membership works, and see the value: scan, pay, earn, redeem — like Starbucks Rewards.

Goal: convince the customer to sign up before entering the festival.
Open Landing
discoverykutafestival.id/
02
Create membership.

Customer signs up with name, email, WhatsApp, and country. Or one-tap with Google.

Required: Nama Lengkap, Email, WhatsApp, Country. Login via Google or password.
Open Sign Up
discoverykutafestival.id/signup
03
Receive your Festival Pass.

A unique QR is generated. This is your digital pass — what merchants scan when you transact, what booth staff scan when you redeem rewards.

QR contains: Member ID, Name, Tier. It is your single identifier across the festival.
Open Dashboard
discoverykutafestival.id/dashboard
04
Visit a merchant.

Customer walks to any UMKM stand and orders. When ready to pay, customer shows the Festival Pass QR. Merchant scans it with their POS app.

Like Starbucks: show your QR, cashier scans, payment request appears on your phone.
See Merchant POS
discoverykutafestival.id/merchant/pos
05
Receive payment request.

Once the merchant scans your QR and enters the bill, a payment request appears on your phone. You see merchant name, amount, and points you will earn.

Passive flow: you do not enter amounts. The merchant does. You just approve.
Open Dashboard Try Demo Modal
discoverykutafestival.id/dashboard
06
Pick payment method, pay.

Choose QRIS, GoPay, OVO, DANA, credit card, or virtual account. Confirm. Payment is processed via Discovery Mall as the merchant of record. Points credit instantly.

Rp 10,000 = 1 pt. Payment routes through Discovery Mall, which settles to UMKM daily.
discoverykutafestival.id/dashboard/pay
07
Earn at events too.

Beyond transactions: daily check-ins, attending events (Sunset Music Session, workshops), and special actions all credit bonus points. Staff scan your QR at event entrances.

Event points: 50 pts per check-in, 5 pts daily, 10 pts registration bonus.
Staff Scanner View
discoverykutafestival.id/staff/scanner
08
Redeem at the booth.

After collecting points, browse the catalogue, pick a reward, then go to the redemption booth. Staff scan your QR to verify identity + balance, then hand over the merchandise.

Tiers: 50 pts entry items, 500+ premium rewards.
See Redemption Flow
discoverykutafestival.id/staff/scanner
End of Member Flow

One QR. Many touchpoints.

The member's QR is the single identifier across every interaction — merchants scan it for payment, redemption staff scan it to verify rewards, event staff scan it for check-in. Every point earned and spent flows to one account.
