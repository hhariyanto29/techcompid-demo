How it unfolds

Customers carry one QR — their Festival Pass. Merchants never scan, never log in. A daily code bridges the two: payment flows through the microsite, points credit automatically, every transaction tracked.

i.
Sign up & receive your Pass
Register with Google or email. Your personal Festival Pass — a unique QR with your name and ID — is generated instantly.
ii.
Show your Pass at merchants
Visit any UMKM stand and present your Festival Pass. The merchant verifies it visually — no scanning, no apps, no logins on their side.
iii.
Pay through the microsite
The merchant gives you a daily code (e.g., "ICE20"). Enter the amount + code in the microsite, pay via QRIS or e-wallet, and points are credited automatically.
iv.
Check in at festival events
At event entrances, festival staff scan your Pass to log attendance and award bonus points — Sunset sessions, workshops, daily check-ins.
v.
Redeem at the booth
Choose from a curated merchandise catalogue. At the redemption booth, staff scan your Pass to verify and complete the redemption.
vi.
Carry it forward
When the festival ends, your membership transitions — into the broader Discovery Mall loyalty universe.
