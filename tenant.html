How payment works
Discovery Mall collects every transaction. We settle to your bank account daily.
i.
Customer shows their Festival Pass QR
You scan it with this POS app. The customer's name and member tier appear on your screen.
ii.
Enter the bill amount, send the payment request
A payment request is pushed to the customer's phone with the amount you entered.
iii.
Customer pays via QRIS, e-wallet, or card
Payment flows to Discovery Mall's merchant account. You'll see the transaction land in real-time on this dashboard.
iv.
Daily payout to your bank account
Discovery Mall settles your transactions every day via bank transfer. Payout schedule visible above.
